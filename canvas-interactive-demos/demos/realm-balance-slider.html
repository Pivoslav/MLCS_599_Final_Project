Realm balance; coupled sliders

This prototype answers: how does it feel when raising one realm necessarily pulls from the others? The main game keeps Order, Reform, and People as separate 0–100 meters; here we use a toy model so sliders stay interesting: a fixed combined total of 180 (each stat still capped at 0–100). Moving one slider redistributes the remainder between the other two in proportion to their previous values. Rounding is applied per step; tiny drift is corrected on input.
