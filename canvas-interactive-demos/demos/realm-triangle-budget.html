Realm triangle — point budget

Pedagogy: scarcity. Players receive a small budget (30 points) to add on top of a neutral baseline (20 each), still respecting the 0–100 cap per meter. This mirrors co-op negotiation: you cannot max everything; the readout shows points left and uses more than color (counts + aria-live).
